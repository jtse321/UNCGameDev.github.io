UNC Chapel Hill's Game Development Club is always accepting new members with any level of experience.

We encourage students outside of the computer science major to join as we teach very low-level game development that requires no prior experience.

The club is completely free to join and there are no required dues! Dues are only necessary if you would like a club T-shirt or would like to annually vote on leadership positions.

Also be sure to swing by our UNC Global Game Jam event that happens every January! You can team up to make your very own games and work with extremely knowledgeable developers. There are also lectures during the event to learn new aspects of development.
