Who are we?

The UNC Chapel Hill's Game Development club was started during the Fall semester of 2018. We have since worked on four games in total! All of our club members have started with little-to-no experience with game development, but we have made plenty of progress. UNC students of all majors are welcome to join our club and take part in what we do. We maintain a very inclusive environment and are willing to help anyone interested in learning game development skills.

The club is completely free to join and there are no required dues! Dues are only necessary if you would like a club T-shirt or would like to annually vote on leadership positions.

UNC Global Game Jam

Make sure to check out our annual Global Game Jam event planned alongside the International Game Developers Association! You can team up or work alone to make your very own games as well as work side-by-side with some of the top developers in the area. You are not required to be a UNC student, but you must be 18 years or older to participate. This is a 48 jam much like a hackathon, but for games. Lectures are also hosted by the our club to help teach new aspects of game development to all that attend.
